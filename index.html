RIGOROUS PROGRAM

Kayenzi spring college offers a rigorous program for nursery,primary,ordinary level (S1-S3) and advanced level (s4-s6).it follow the Rwanda national curriculum (REB),infusing the student-centered teaching strategies,designed to develop critical thinking and Leadership skills.our A' level program is afocused to offers the following combination MEG( Mathematics Economics Geography), MEC( Mathematics Economics Computer-science) ,HEG( History Economics Geography),HEL(History Economics Literature),LFK(Literature French Kinyarwanda)

using the Rwandan national curriculum as foundation we use 21st century eductional practices that focus on devoloping habits of mind, habits of academics and habbits of success. we are nurturing the next generation of Rwandan leaders and changemarkers,by providing them with an opporturity to become well versed in following 21st century competencies:

Grith & Growth Mindset(GGM)

- The value of perserverance ,acquiring knowledge ,skills,and understanding through practive and experience

social consciousness & Gratitude

appreciation for the direct and indirect work of other within one's network and acknowledgment of luck in the outcomes.striving to solve problems that matter for those who are less fortunate
